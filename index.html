The Reality

Every Building Project Has a Specification.
Is Your Product In It?

Holmes Sales Group partners with building product manufacturers to drive specification, sales, and market expansion.

We work as an extension of your team, securing product placement in architectural specifications through focused A&D outreach and presentations, supporting the path from spec to purchase, and helping you strengthen market coverage.

In addition, we identify and place proven A&D and territory sales representatives who understand your product, your channel, and your regional goals.

Our role is simple: help manufacturers get in the specification, create product awareness with architects plus general contractors, and get more sales.

Every manufacturer is different, so our approach is never one-size-fits-all.

Our Approach
We believe relationships matter. Trust matters. Follow-through matters. We focus on long-term growth, not quick wins. When your product is specified, approved, and installed that's success to us.
